Syntax of style rule
Selector{property 1:value 1;property 2:value 2;...}
h1 {font-size:12pt;color:red}
-should be separated by semicolon

order of precedence
-inline style sheet
-embedded style sheet
-external style sheet
-browser default

Inline style
    Style="property0:value0;property1:value1;

External style sheet    
    <link href=URL rel="relation_type="link_type">
